Jerkies are a set of [Food](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Food) items added in FlashFun.<br>
Salted and dried meats that provide high saturation. Each variant uses the same shapeless recipe - combine the corresponding cooked meat with Salt.

All jerkies are crafted **shapeless** using: the corresponding cooked meat + Salt.
