The Enchanted Golden Apple is a [Food](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Food) item added in FlashFun.<br>
A craftable version of the legendary fruit. Functions identically to the vanilla enchanted golden apple - Absorption, Regeneration, Fire Resistance, and Resistance all in one.

## Enchanted Golden Apple

- **Effects on eat:**
  - Absorption IV
  - Regeneration II
  - Fire Resistance
  - Resistance
- **Unlock Level:** 30

### Recipe

|-|-|-|
| 24 Carat Block | 24 Carat Block | 24 Carat Block |
| 24 Carat Block | Apple | 24 Carat Block |
| 24 Carat Block | 24 Carat Block | 24 Carat Block |
