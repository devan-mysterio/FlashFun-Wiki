Monster Jerky is a [Food](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Food) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Rotten flesh that has been salt-cured into something actually edible. Removes the Hunger effect when eaten.

## Monster Jerky

- **Effect:** Removes the Hunger potion effect on eat
- **Saturation:** High (6 saturation points)
- **Unlock Level:** 8
