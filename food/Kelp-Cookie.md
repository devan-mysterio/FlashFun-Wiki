The Kelp Cookie is a [Food](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Food) item added in FlashFun.<br>
An ocean-flavored cookie infused with dried kelp. Eating it lets you breathe underwater for a short time.

## Kelp Cookie

- **Effect:** Grants Water Breathing for 15 seconds on eat
- **Saturation:** High (6 saturation points)
- **Unlock Level:** 3
