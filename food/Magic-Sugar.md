Magic Sugar is a [Food](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Food) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Sugar infused with redstone and glowstone. Right-clicking it - not eating it - grants a brief but significant burst of speed.

## Magic Sugar

- **Usage:** Right-click to consume
- **Effect:** Grants Speed II for 15 seconds
- **Unlock Level:** 10

### Recipe (Shapeless)

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Sugar | Redstone | Glowstone Dust |

*It's sugar. With redstone. And glowstone. The science is unclear but the speed is real.*
