The Fortune Cookie is a [Food](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Food) item added in FlashFun.<br>
A cookie sealed with a hidden message. Eat it and receive a random piece of wisdom - or something like it.

## Fortune Cookie

- **Effect:** Displays a random fortune quote in chat when eaten
- **Unlock Level:** 1
