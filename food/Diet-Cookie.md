The Diet Cookie is a [Food](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Food) item added in FlashFun.<br>
A featherlight cookie made with elytra material. Eating it causes you to fall more slowly for a short time.

## Diet Cookie

- **Effect:** Grants Slow Falling on eat
- **Unlock Level:** 15
