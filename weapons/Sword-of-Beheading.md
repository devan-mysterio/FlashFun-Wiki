The Sword of Beheading is a [Weapon](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Weapons) added in FlashFun.<br>
Gives a 40% chance to drop a skull when killing certain mobs. Supported mobs: Skeletons, Wither Skeletons, Zombies, and Creepers.



## Sword of Beheading

- **Attack Damage:** +8.0
- **Attack Speed:** -2.4
- **Skull Drop Chance:** 40%
- **Unlock Level:** 8
