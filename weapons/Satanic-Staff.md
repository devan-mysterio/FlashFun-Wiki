The Satanic Staff is a [Weapon](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Weapons) added in FlashFun.<br>
Fires a sonic boom projectile that travels up to 40 blocks, detecting entities in a 2-block radius and dealing 150 damage. Unbreakable. Fire immune.



## Satanic Staff

- **Ability:** Fires a sonic boom projectile on right-click
- **Range:** 40 blocks
- **Detection Radius:** 2 blocks
- **Damage:** 150
- **Cooldown:** 60 seconds
- **Unbreakable:** Yes
- **Fire Immune:** Yes
- **Unlock Level:** 15

### Recipe (Shapeless)

- Demonic Token
- Infernal Essence
- Trident
