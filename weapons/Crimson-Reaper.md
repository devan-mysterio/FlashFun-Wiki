The Crimson Reaper is a [Weapon](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Weapons) added in FlashFun.<br>
A bloodthirsty blade that has a 45% chance to steal life from enemies on hit, healing the attacker for 2 hearts.



## Crimson Reaper

- **Attack Damage:** +8.0
- **Attack Speed:** -2.4
- **Life Steal Chance:** 45%
- **Life Steal Amount:** 4 HP (2 hearts), capped at max health
- **Enchantments:** Sharpness II, Fire Aspect II, Unbreaking IV
- **Unlock Level:** 10
