Energy and Electricity is a category in [FlashFun](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Flash-Networks-in-a-Nutshell) containing machines, generators, capacitors, and wiring for building electric power networks.

## How Energy Networks Work

All electric machines require a connected energy network to operate. A basic network consists of:

1. **Energy Regulator** - the controller (one per network)
2. **Energy Connectors** - cables connecting the regulator to storage
3. **Capacitors** - energy storage units
4. **Generators** - produce energy and push it into capacitors
5. **Machines** - consume energy from capacitors

Generators and machines must be placed within 6 blocks of a capacitor or connector to interact with the network.
