Reporting bugs is important so that FlashFun can keep improving.<br>
So please consider reporting them - Devan actually reads them, pinky promise.

_Also see: [Common Issues](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Common-Issues)_

## Before You Report

Before firing off a bug report, ask yourself the following questions:

* **Did you read the wiki?** You're already here, so good start.
* **Is the server actually running?** You'd be surprised.
* **Have you tried turning it off and on again?** Relog. Seriously. Just try it.
* **Is it actually a bug, or did you just not know what you were doing?** No judgement. But worth checking.

If after all that it's still broken - great, that's probably a real bug. Let's get it fixed.

## How to Report

There are two ways to reach Devan:

### Option 1 - Open a Ticket in Discord
[Join the Flash-Networks Discord](https://discord.gg/WTspjB2b6g) and open a support ticket.<br>
This is the preferred method as it keeps things organized and lets others help too.

### Option 2 - Direct Message Devan
Find **@devanmysterio** on Discord and slide into his DMs.<br>
He will see it. Eventually. He is one person.

## What to Include

Whether you open a ticket or DM directly, please include:

* **What happened** - describe the bug as clearly as you can
* **What you expected to happen** - maybe it's working as intended, who knows
* **How to reproduce it** - step by step if possible
* **Any screenshots or video** - a picture is worth a thousand bug reports

The more detail you provide, the faster it gets fixed. Vague reports get vague responses.
