Processing Machines are part of [Energy and Electricity](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Energy-and-Electricity) in FlashFun. They consume energy from a connected network to perform various processing tasks. Connect to an energy network to power.

## Charging Bench

- **Unlock Level:** 22
- **Buffer:** 128 J
- **Charges items at:** 5 J/s
- **Energy cost:** 10 J/s (50% efficient)
- **Note:** Right-click to open charging interface.
