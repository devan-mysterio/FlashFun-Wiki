Reactors are part of [Energy and Electricity](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Energy-and-Electricity) in FlashFun. They are the highest-output energy generators in the game and require careful setup.

**Both reactors require:**
- Placement submerged in a **3x3x3 water cube**
- Active coolant cells to operate
- **⚠ Explode if water or coolant is removed!**

## Nuclear Reactor

- **Unlock Level:** 45
- **Output:** 500 J/s
- **Buffer:** 16,384 J
- **Fuels:** Boosted Uranium, Uranium, Neptunium, Plutonium
- **Byproduct:** Neptunium → Plutonium
- **Coolant:** Reactor Coolant Cells (consumed every 10s)
- **Setup:** Must be submerged in a 3x3x3 water cube
- **Note:** Requires a [Reactor Access Port](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Reactors#reactor-access-port) to interface with cargo systems

## Nether Star Reactor

- **Unlock Level:** 50
- **Output:** 1,024 J/s
- **Buffer:** 32,768 J
- **Fuel:** Nether Star (30 min · 1.84 MJ)
- **Coolant:** Nether Ice Coolant Cells (consumed every 10s)
- **Setup:** Must be submerged in a 3x3x3 water cube
- **⚠ Warning:** Withers nearby unprotected entities while active

## Reactor Access Port

The Reactor Access Port is an optional companion block that allows cargo nodes to interact with a reactor. It is crafted as part of the Cargo Management category (Unlock Level 38).

- **Function:** Interface for cargo systems
- **Setup:** Place 3 blocks above a reactor
- **Allows:** Cargo nodes to import fuel and coolant, export byproducts
- **Acts as:** A buffer between the reactor and network

*500 J/s of clean energy, or one moment of inattention. Keep the water full.*
