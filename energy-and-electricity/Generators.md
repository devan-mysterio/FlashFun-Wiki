Generators are part of [Energy and Electricity](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Energy-and-Electricity) in FlashFun. They produce energy and push it into capacitors. Place adjacent to a Capacitor or within 6 blocks of an Energy Connector.

## Solar Generator

- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Output:** 4 J/s
- **Buffer:** None
- **Works:** Daytime only
