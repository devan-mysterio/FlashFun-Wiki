Wiring components are part of [Energy and Electricity](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Energy-and-Electricity) in FlashFun. They form the backbone of any energy network.

## Energy Regulator

- **Unlock Level:** 22
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Function:** Network controller - required to start an energy network
- **Note:** One per network. Scans up to 6 blocks in all directions and connects to Energy Connectors.

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Silver Ingot | Damascus Steel Ingot | Silver Ingot |
| Damascus Steel Ingot | Electric Motor | Damascus Steel Ingot |
| Silver Ingot | Damascus Steel Ingot | Silver Ingot |

## Energy Connector

- **Unlock Level:** 18
- **Yield:** 8 per craft
- **Function:** Energy cable connecting the regulator to capacitors
- **Note:** Place within 6 blocks of an Energy Regulator.

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Carbon | Copper Wire | Carbon |
| Carbon | Redstone Block | Carbon |
| Carbon | Copper Wire | Carbon |

*One regulator per network. This is not a suggestion.*
