Capacitors are part of [Energy and Electricity](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Energy-and-Electricity) in FlashFun. They store energy on a network. Place within 6 blocks of an Energy Connector.

Each tier upgrades into the next - the previous tier capacitor is used as a crafting ingredient.

## Small Energy Capacitor

- **Unlock Level:** 22
- **Capacity:** 128 J

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Duralumin Ingot | Sulfate | Duralumin Ingot |
| Redstone Alloy Ingot | Energy Connector | Redstone Alloy Ingot |
| Duralumin Ingot | Redstone Dust | Duralumin Ingot |
