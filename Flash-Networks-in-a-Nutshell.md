# Welcome to the FlashFun Wiki

**FlashFun** is a custom Paper plugin for [Flash-Networks](https://www.discord.gg/WTspjB2b6g) - a public Minecraft server and passion project maintained by **Devan Mysterio** since 2015 (formerly known as *DoctorDisco*).

FlashFun aims to make Flash-Networks feel like a modpack - adding hundreds of new items, machines, minions, and crafting systems entirely through a server-side plugin, with no mods required.



## What is FlashFun?

FlashFun adds a massive layer of content on top of vanilla Minecraft survival - custom crafted items, electric machines, automated minions, ritual magic, cargo transport systems, tiered backpacks, and much more.

Some highlights include:

- **Backpacks** - Tiered portable storage, including an Ender-linked variant
- **Electric Machines** - Generators, processors, reactors, and more
- **Automated Minions** - 11 types of workers that mine, smelt, drill, refine, and produce
- **Cargo Networks** - Automated item transport systems between machines
- **GPS & Portal Systems** - Satellite teleporters and wormhole Portal Guns
- **Armor Sets** - Satanic, Ender, Hazmat, Novelty, and Elite armor
- **Ritual Crafting** - Ground-based casting by throwing items into a summoning circle
- **Weapons & Gadgets** - Explosive bows, grappling hooks, jetpacks, staffs, and more
- **Enchantment Runes** - Apply custom enchantments outside the enchanting table
- **Recipe Guide** - An in-game guide to browse and unlock crafting recipes



## Why FlashFun?

In Q4 2025, the Slimefun project was discontinued. Rather than abandon the style of gameplay that made Flash-Networks unique, Devan rebuilt the entire experience from scratch - solo - in **130 days**.

FlashFun is not a fork or a port. It is a ground-up reimagination, custom-built specifically for Flash-Networks.



## Flash-Network's Story

Flash-Networks started in **early 2015** as a small community server called *TheVortex*, founded by Devan Mysterio as a personal passion project. Minecraft plugin development began in **2016**, and by **2020** the server was rebranded to **Flash-Networks** - a name it carries to this day.

For years, Slimefun served as a backbone of the server's custom content - providing hundreds of items, machines, and systems that transformed the vanilla survival experience into something more akin to a modpack.

When Slimefun was discontinued in **Q4 2025**, Devan made the decision to build a replacement entirely from scratch. Over the next **130 days**, FlashFun was developed solo: a custom crafting altar, ritual system, 11 minion types, 20+ electric machines, a cargo network, GPS teleportation, and hundreds of unique items - all rebuilt and redesigned for Flash-Networks.

FlashFun is not a public release. It lives exclusively on Flash-Networks, built with love for a small community that's been around for a long time.
