Talisman of the Water Breather is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
When you run out of air underwater, this talisman activates and grants Water Breathing before you drown.

## Talisman of the Water Breather

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Trigger:** Air meter reaching zero underwater
- **Effect:** Grants Water Breathing for 5 minutes, restores air meter
- **Note:** Does not activate if you already have Water Breathing.
