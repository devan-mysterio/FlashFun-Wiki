Talisman of the Wise is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Every time you gain XP, there's a 20% chance that the amount is doubled. Works passively from inventory.

## Talisman of the Wise

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 25
- **Trigger:** Gaining XP from any source
- **Effect:** 20% chance to double XP gained
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack. Requires [Talisman of the Magician](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Magician) to craft.
