Talisman of the Traveller is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
When you sprint, there's a 60% chance to receive a brief burst of Speed II. Works passively from inventory.

## Talisman of the Traveller

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 25
- **Trigger:** Toggling sprint
- **Effect:** 60% chance to grant Speed II for 10 seconds
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack. Requires [Talisman of the Angel](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Angel) to craft.
