Talisman of the Miner is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Each ore broken has a 20% chance to drop a duplicate of its yield. Works passively from inventory.

## Talisman of the Miner

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Trigger:** Breaking any ore block
- **Effect:** 20% chance to double ore drops
- **Note:** Does not trigger with Silk Touch. Multiple copies do not stack.

Works on: Coal, Iron, Copper, Gold, Redstone, Emerald, Lapis, Diamond (regular and deepslate), Nether Gold, Nether Quartz, Ancient Debris
