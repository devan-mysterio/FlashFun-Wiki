Talisman of the Firefighter is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
When you catch fire, this talisman activates and grants Fire Resistance - putting out the flames.

## Talisman of the Firefighter

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Trigger:** Taking fire or fire tick damage
- **Effect:** Grants Fire Resistance for 5 minutes, cancels the triggering damage
- **Note:** Does not activate if you already have Fire Resistance, or if wearing Satanic/Hazmat armor.
