Talisman of the Hunter is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Killing a mob has a 20% chance to duplicate all of its drops. Works passively from inventory.

## Talisman of the Hunter

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Trigger:** Killing any mob
- **Effect:** 20% chance to double all mob drops
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack.
