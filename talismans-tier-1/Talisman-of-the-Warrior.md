Talisman of the Warrior is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Every time you take damage, this talisman fires back - granting Strength III for 10 seconds and then consuming itself.

## Talisman of the Warrior

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 22
- **Trigger:** Taking any damage
- **Effect:** Grants Strength III for 10 seconds
- **Note:** Consumed on every hit taken.
