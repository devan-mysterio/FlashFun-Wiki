Talisman of the Angel is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Taking fall damage has a 75% chance to be fully negated. Works passively from inventory.

## Talisman of the Angel

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Trigger:** Taking fall damage
- **Effect:** 75% chance to cancel the fall damage entirely
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack.
