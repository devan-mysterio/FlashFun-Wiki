Talisman of the Magician is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
When enchanting at an enchanting table, there's an 80% chance to receive a completely random bonus enchantment on top of the normal result.

## Talisman of the Magician

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 22
- **Trigger:** Enchanting an item at an enchanting table
- **Effect:** 80% chance to add one random bonus enchantment
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack. The bonus enchant is random - any valid enchant for the item.
