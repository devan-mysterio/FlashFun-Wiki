Talisman of the Caveman is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Breaking an ore block has a 50% chance to trigger Haste II for 6 seconds. Works passively from inventory.

## Talisman of the Caveman

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 25
- **Trigger:** Breaking any ore block or bedrock
- **Effect:** 50% chance to grant Haste II for 6 seconds
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack. Requires [Talisman of the Miner](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Miner) to craft.

Works on: All ores (including deepslate variants), Nether Gold, Nether Quartz, Ancient Debris, Bedrock
