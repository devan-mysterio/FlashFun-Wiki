Talisman of the Whirlwind is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Incoming projectiles have a 60% chance to be deflected entirely. Works passively from inventory.

## Talisman of the Whirlwind

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 27
- **Trigger:** Taking projectile damage
- **Effect:** 60% chance to cancel the projectile hit
- **Note:** Tridents pierce this protection and are not deflected. Multiple copies do not stack. Requires [Talisman of the Traveller](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Traveller) to craft.
