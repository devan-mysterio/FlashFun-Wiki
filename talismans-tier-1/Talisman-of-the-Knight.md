Talisman of the Knight is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
When you take damage, there's a 30% chance this talisman activates - granting Regeneration II for 5 seconds and then consuming itself.

## Talisman of the Knight

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 25
- **Trigger:** Taking any damage
- **Effect:** 30% chance to grant Regeneration II for 5 seconds
- **Note:** Consumed on activation. Requires [Talisman of the Warrior](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Warrior) to craft.
