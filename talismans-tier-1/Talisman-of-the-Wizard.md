Talisman of the Wizard is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
When enchanting a tool or weapon that can receive Fortune, this talisman forces Fortune IV or V onto the result - though it may weaken another enchantment in the process.

## Talisman of the Wizard

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 30
- **Trigger:** Enchanting an item that can receive Fortune
- **Effect:** Forces Fortune IV or V on the enchanted item
- **Risk:** 40% chance to reduce one other enchantment by 1 level
- **Note:** Does not work on Satanic tools (Satanic Pickaxe, Shovel, Hoe). Requires [Talisman of the Magician](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Magician) to craft.
