Talisman of the Anvil is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
When a tool in your inventory is about to break, this talisman activates and resets its durability - saving it from destruction.

## Talisman of the Anvil

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Trigger:** Tool reaches 0 durability
- **Effect:** Restores the tool to near-full durability
- **Note:** Does not work on Satanic armor pieces
