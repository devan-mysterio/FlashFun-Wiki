Talisman of the Farmer is a [Talismans (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Harvesting fully-grown crops has a 20% chance to drop a duplicate yield. Works passively from inventory.

## Talisman of the Farmer

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Trigger:** Breaking a fully-grown crop
- **Effect:** 20% chance to double crop drops
- **Note:** Only triggers on mature crops. Multiple copies do not stack.

Works on: Wheat, Carrots, Potatoes, Beetroots, Nether Wart, Cocoa, Sweet Berries, Melon, Pumpkin, Sugar Cane, Bamboo, Cactus
