GPS-Based Machines is a category in [FlashFun](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Flash-Networks-in-a-Nutshell) containing teleportation structures, portable scanning tools, and the Portal Gun.

## GPS Teleporter

A multiblock teleportation structure. Build one, step on the plate, and go somewhere else entirely.

| Item | Unlock Level | Description |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| [GPS Control Panel](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Teleporter#gps-control-panel) | 26 | Owns and configures the GPS structure |
| [GPS Teleport Pylon](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Teleporter#gps-teleport-pylon) | 22 | Energy ring component - place 8 around the matrix (x8 per craft) |
| [GPS Teleporter Matrix](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Teleporter#gps-teleporter-matrix) | 30 | Core multiblock block |
| [GPS Activation Matrix](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Teleporter#gps-activation-matrix) | 28 | Pressure plate - step on to activate teleport |
