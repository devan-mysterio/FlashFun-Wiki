The Vitamin Tier 3 is a [Useful Item](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Useful-Items) added in FlashFun.<br>
A potent health supplement packed with curative properties. The top-tier vitamin, curing most ailments on use.



## Vitamin Tier 3

- **Healing:** Restores 4 hearts
- **Extinguishes fire** on use
- **Cures** Poison and Wither
- **Removes** all other negative effects
- **Usage:** Right-click to use
- **Stack Size:** 8
- **Unlock Level:** 5

### Recipe

Crafted shapeless using the following items:

- Tin Can
- Apple
- Sugar
- Red Mushroom

**Output:** 1 Vitamin Tier 3



*A tin can full of mushroom, apple, and sugar. Your doctor would be horrified. Your health bar will not.*
