The Portable Dustbin is a [Useful Item](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Useful-Items) added in FlashFun.<br>
A pocket-sized trash can. Throw your garbage away from anywhere - items placed inside are deleted permanently.



## Portable Dustbin

- **Effect:** Opens a trash can GUI on right-click - items placed inside are permanently deleted
- **Usage:** Right-click to open
- **Stack Size:** 1
- **Unlock Level:** 3
