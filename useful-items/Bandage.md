The Bandage is a [Useful Item](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Useful-Items) added in FlashFun.<br>
An advanced medical supply crafted from rags. Restores more health than a rag and puts out fires.



## Bandage

- **Healing:** Restores 4 hearts
- **Extinguishes fire** on use
- **Usage:** Right-click to use
- **Stack Size:** 4
- **Unlock Level:** 2

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Rag | String | Rag |

**Output:** 4 Bandages



*Twice the rag, twice the results. Science.*
