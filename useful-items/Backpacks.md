Backpacks are a series of [Useful Items](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Useful-Items) added in FlashFun.<br>
Portable storage containers that you can open anywhere by right-clicking. Each tier upgrades the previous one, adding more storage slots.

Each backpack has a unique UUID that ties its storage to that specific item - two backpacks of the same tier have separate inventories.

## Leather Backpack (Tier 1)

- **Storage:** 9 slots
- **Usage:** Right-click to open
- **Unlock Level:** 5

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Leather | - | Leather |
| 6-Carat Gold Bar | Chest | 6-Carat Gold Bar |
| Leather | Leather | Leather |
