The Ender Backpack is a [Useful Item](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Useful-Items) added in FlashFun.<br>
A portal to your ender chest. Opens your personal ender chest inventory from anywhere in the world.



## Ender Backpack

- **Effect:** Opens your personal ender chest inventory on right-click
- **Linked to:** The player who crafted it (displayed on the item)
- **Usage:** Right-click to open
- **Unlock Level:** 20

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Ender Lump Tier III | Leather | Ender Lump Tier III |
| Leather | Ender Chest | Leather |
| Ender Lump Tier III | Leather | Ender Lump Tier III |



*It's just your ender chest. In your pocket. Wrapped in leather and magical void energy.*
