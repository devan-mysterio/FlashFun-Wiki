The Cooler is a [Useful Item](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Useful-Items) added in FlashFun.<br>
A refrigerated portable storage container that only accepts food. Automatically feeds you when you're hungry.



## Cooler

- **Storage:** 27 slots (food items only)
- **Auto-feed:** Automatically feeds you when your hunger drops
- **Usage:** Right-click to open
- **Unlock Level:** 15

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Cloth | Cloth | Cloth |
| Aluminum Ingot | Cooling Unit | Aluminum Ingot |
| Aluminum Ingot | Aluminum Ingot | Aluminum Ingot |

> The **Cooling Unit** is a Technical Component crafted from Ice, Aluminum Ingots, and an Electric Motor.



*It feeds you automatically. You literally cannot starve if you remember to fill it.*
