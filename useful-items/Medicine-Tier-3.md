The Medicine Tier 3 is a [Useful Item](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Useful-Items) added in FlashFun.<br>
A powerful healing elixir brewed from a Vitamin Tier 3. Shares the same effects but comes in liquid form.



## Medicine Tier 3

- **Healing:** Restores 4 hearts
- **Extinguishes fire** on use
- **Cures** Poison and Wither
- **Removes** all other negative effects
- **Usage:** Right-click to drink
- **Stack Size:** 1
- **Unlock Level:** 7

### Recipe

Crafted shapeless using the following items:

- [Vitamin Tier 3](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/useful-items/Vitamin-Tier-3)
- Snowball
- Glass Bottle

**Output:** 1 Medicine Tier 3



*You dissolved vitamins in snowmelt and put it in a bottle. That's either medicine or a very bad cocktail.*
