The Ender Talisman of the Whirlwind is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Whirlwind](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Whirlwind). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Whirlwind

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 34
- **Trigger:** Taking projectile damage
- **Effect:** 60% chance to cancel the projectile hit
- **Note:** Tridents pierce this protection. Multiple copies do not stack.
