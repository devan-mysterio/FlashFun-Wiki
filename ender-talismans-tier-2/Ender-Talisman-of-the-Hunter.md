The Ender Talisman of the Hunter is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Hunter](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Hunter). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Hunter

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 30
- **Trigger:** Killing any mob
- **Effect:** 20% chance to double all mob drops
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack.
