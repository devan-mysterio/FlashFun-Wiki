The Ender Talisman of the Firefighter is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Firefighter](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Firefighter). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Firefighter

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 30
- **Trigger:** Taking fire or fire tick damage
- **Effect:** Grants Fire Resistance for 5 minutes, cancels the triggering damage
- **Note:** Does not activate if you already have Fire Resistance or wear Satanic/Hazmat armor. Ender Chest stack consumed first.
