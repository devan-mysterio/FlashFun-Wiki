The Ender Talisman of the Wizard is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Wizard](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Wizard). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Wizard

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 35
- **Trigger:** Enchanting an item that can receive Fortune
- **Effect:** Forces Fortune IV or V on the enchanted item
- **Risk:** 40% chance to reduce one other enchantment by 1 level
- **Note:** Does not work on Satanic tools.
