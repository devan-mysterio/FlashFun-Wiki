The Ender Talisman of the Traveller is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Traveller](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Traveller). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Traveller

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 32
- **Trigger:** Toggling sprint
- **Effect:** 60% chance to grant Speed II for 10 seconds
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack.
