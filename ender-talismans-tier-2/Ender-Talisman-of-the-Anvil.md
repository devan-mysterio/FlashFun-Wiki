The Ender Talisman of the Anvil is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Anvil](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Anvil). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Anvil

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 30
- **Trigger:** Tool reaches 0 durability
- **Effect:** Restores the tool to near-full durability
- **Note:** Does not work on Satanic armor pieces. Ender Chest stack consumed first.
