The Ender Talisman of the Water Breather is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Water Breather](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Water-Breather). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Water Breather

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 30
- **Trigger:** Air meter reaching zero underwater
- **Effect:** Grants Water Breathing for 5 minutes, restores air meter
- **Note:** Does not activate if you already have Water Breathing. Ender Chest stack consumed first.
