The Ender Talisman of the Knight is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Knight](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Knight). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Knight

- **Type:** Consumable
- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 32
- **Trigger:** Taking any damage
- **Effect:** 30% chance to grant Regeneration II for 5 seconds
- **Note:** Consumed on activation. Ender Chest stack consumed first.
