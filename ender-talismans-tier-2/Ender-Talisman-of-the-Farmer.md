The Ender Talisman of the Farmer is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Farmer](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Farmer). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Farmer

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 30
- **Trigger:** Breaking a fully-grown crop
- **Effect:** 20% chance to double crop drops
- **Note:** Only triggers on mature crops. Multiple copies do not stack.
