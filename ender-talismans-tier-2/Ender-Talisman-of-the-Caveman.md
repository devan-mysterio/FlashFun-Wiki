The Ender Talisman of the Caveman is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Caveman](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Caveman). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Caveman

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 32
- **Trigger:** Breaking any ore block or bedrock
- **Effect:** 50% chance to grant Haste II for 6 seconds
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack.
