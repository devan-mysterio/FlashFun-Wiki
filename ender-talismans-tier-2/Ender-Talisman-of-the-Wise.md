The Ender Talisman of the Wise is an [Ender Talisman (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) added in FlashFun.<br>
Ender-infused upgrade of the [Talisman of the Wise](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talisman-of-the-Wise). Same effect - activates from inventory or Ender Chest.

## Ender Talisman of the Wise

- **Type:** Passive
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 32
- **Trigger:** Gaining XP from any source
- **Effect:** 20% chance to double XP gained
- **Note:** Multiple copies do not stack.
