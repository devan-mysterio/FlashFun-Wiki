Food items are consumables added in FlashFun that provide enhanced effects beyond vanilla food.<br>
Most are crafted by preserving or enhancing existing ingredients with FlashFun materials.

| Item | Unlock Level | Effect |
|-|-|-|
| [Fortune Cookie](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Fortune-Cookie) | 1 | Displays a random quote on eat |
| [Kelp Cookie](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Kelp-Cookie) | 3 | Grants Water Breathing for 15 seconds |
| [Jerkies](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Jerkies) | 5 | High-saturation dried meats (6 variants + fish + salmon) |
| [Monster Jerky](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Monster-Jerky) | 8 | Removes Hunger effect on eat |
| [Magic Sugar](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magic-Sugar) | 10 | Right-click to gain Speed II for 15 seconds |
| [Diet Cookie](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Diet-Cookie) | 15 | Grants Slow Falling on eat |
| [Enchanted Golden Apple](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Enchanted-Golden-Apple) | 30 | Craftable version - Absorption IV, Regeneration II, Fire Resistance, Resistance |
