Magical is a category in [FlashFun](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Flash-Networks-in-a-Nutshell) containing arcane crafting materials, elemental staffs, flight items, and utility spells crafted from magical essence.

## Magical Lumps

The core ingredient chain for all magical crafting. Both magical and ender lumps compress upward through three tiers.

| Item | Unlock Level | Description |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| [Magical Lump (Tier 1)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Lumps#magical-lump-tier-1) | 1 | Base magical essence - 1 Nether Wart |
| [Magical Lump (Tier 2)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Lumps#magical-lump-tier-2) | 3 | 4x Tier 1 |
| [Magical Lump (Tier 3)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Lumps#magical-lump-tier-3) | 6 | 4x Tier 2 |
| [Ender Lump (Tier 1)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Lumps#ender-lump-tier-1) | 2 | Base ender essence - 1 Ender Eye |
| [Ender Lump (Tier 2)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Lumps#ender-lump-tier-2) | 4 | 4x Tier 1 |
| [Ender Lump (Tier 3)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Lumps#ender-lump-tier-3) | 7 | 4x Tier 2 |
