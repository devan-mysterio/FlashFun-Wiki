The Gold Pan is a [Tool](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Tools) added in FlashFun.<br>
A prospector's essential tool. Right-click gravel blocks to sift through them for hidden treasures.

## Gold Pan

- **Usage:** Right-click a gravel block to pan it
- **Unlock Level:** 2
