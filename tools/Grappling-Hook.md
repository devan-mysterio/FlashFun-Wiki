The Grappling Hook is a [Tool](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Tools) added in FlashFun.<br>
A hook-and-chain device that lets you swing through the world. Cast it at a surface and pull yourself toward it.

## Grappling Hook

- **Usage:** Right-click to cast the hook, right-click again to pull yourself toward it
- **Unlock Level:** 12
