The Pickaxe of Containment is a [Tool](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Tools) added in FlashFun.<br>
A pickaxe engineered to harvest monster spawners intact. Break a spawner with it and it drops as a usable item, preserving its mob type.

## Pickaxe of Containment

- **Effect:** Breaks spawners and drops them as a collectable item with their mob type preserved
- **No experience** dropped when breaking spawners
- **Unlock Level:** 18
