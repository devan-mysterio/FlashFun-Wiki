The Pickaxe of the Seeker is a [Tool](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Tools) added in FlashFun.<br>
A compass-fused pickaxe that senses nearby ores. Right-click to locate the nearest valuable ore within 8 blocks.

## Pickaxe of the Seeker

- **Effect:** Right-click to detect and point toward the nearest ore within 8 blocks
- **Detects** all valuable ores
- **Unlock Level:** 26
