The Nether Gold Pan is a [Tool](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Tools) added in FlashFun.<br>
A heat-resistant version of the Gold Pan built for the Nether. Right-click soul sand or soul soil to sift for nether treasures.

## Nether Gold Pan

- **Usage:** Right-click soul sand or soul soil to pan it
- **Unlock Level:** 10
