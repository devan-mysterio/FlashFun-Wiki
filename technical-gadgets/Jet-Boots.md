Jet Boots are a [Technical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Technical-Gadgets) added in FlashFun.<br>
Worn in the boots slot. Hold sneak while on the ground to blast forward at high speed. Accuracy affects how straight you travel - lower tiers drift sideways.

The Armored Jet Boots use an Iron Boots base, providing full boots armor protection alongside the speed boost.

## Usage

Equip in the boots slot. Hold **sneak while standing on the ground** to activate the boost. Cloud particles emit below your feet while boosting.
Lower accuracy tiers add random noise to your horizontal velocity - you may veer off course. T7 is essentially perfect accuracy.
