The Electric Jetpack is a [Technical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Technical-Gadgets) added in FlashFun.<br>
Worn in the chestplate slot. Hold sneak to fire the thrusters and propel yourself upward. Consumes 1 J/s while thrusting.

The Armored Jetpack uses an Iron Chestplate base, providing full chestplate armor protection alongside flight.

## Usage

Equip in the chestplate slot. Hold **sneak** to activate thrust. Cloud particles emit from below while thrusting.
Charge depletes at 1 J/s while active. When charge runs out, the jetpack stops working until recharged.
