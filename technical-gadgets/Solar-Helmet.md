The Solar Helmet is a [Technical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Technical-Gadgets) added in FlashFun.<br>
A helmet that harvests sunlight to passively charge all tech gadgets in your armor slots and hands.

## Solar Helmet

- **Unlock Level:** 26
- **Slot:** Helmet

## Usage

Equip in the helmet slot. Every 2 seconds, if conditions are met, +1 J is added to every chargeable item equipped (armor slots + main hand + off hand).

**Conditions required:**
- Direct sunlight (sky light level ≥ 12)
- Daytime only (before time 12000 - before dusk)
- No thunderstorm

Charging is blocked by roofs, overhangs, rain, and night. Charges up to 0.5 J/s per item when conditions are met.
