Night Vision Goggles are a [Technical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Technical-Gadgets) added in FlashFun.<br>
A helmet-slot item that provides permanent Night Vision while worn. No charge required.

## Night Vision Goggles

- **Unlock Level:** 20
- **Slot:** Helmet
- **No charge required**

## Usage

Equip in the helmet slot. Night Vision is applied automatically and refreshed every 5 seconds.
Provides continuous night vision as long as the goggles are worn.
