The Parachute is a [Technical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Technical-Gadgets) added in FlashFun.<br>
A deployable gliding device worn in the chestplate slot. Hold sneak while falling to deploy it and descend safely.

## Parachute

- **Unlock Level:** 16
- **Slot:** Chestplate
- **No charge required**

## Usage

Equip in the chestplate slot. While falling, hold **sneak** to deploy. While deployed:
- Fall speed is capped at -0.1 m/s
- Fall damage is fully cancelled

Release sneak or land to stop gliding. Cloud particles emit above you while deployed.
