Farmer Shoes are a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Tread lightly upon the harvest. These boots prevent you from trampling farmland - your crops remain safe no matter how hard you stomp.

## Farmer Shoes

- **Armor:** +1
- **Effect:** Walking on farmland does not trample crops
- **Unlock Level:** 5
