Ender Armor is a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) set added in FlashFun.<br>
Woven from the fabric between dimensions. The boots alone cancel ender pearl landing damage - the full set adds more dangerous perks.

## Set Bonus (Full Set)

- **Immune to ender pearl landing damage**
- **Erratic teleportation** during rainstorms (like an enderman)

## Boots (Alone)

- Cancels ender pearl landing damage without needing the full set
