The Boots of the Stomper are a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) item added in FlashFun.<br>
The ground trembles beneath your feet. Land from a fall of 3+ blocks and unleash a seismic shockwave - blocks fly, enemies launch skyward.

## Boots of the Stomper

- **Armor:** +2
- **Effect:** Absorbs all fall damage (3+ block falls trigger the stomp)
- **On stomp:** Seismic wave radiates in a 7-block radius, lifting surface blocks and launching nearby entities upward
- **Damage:** Deals 4 damage to nearby non-player entities
- **Unlock Level:** 10
