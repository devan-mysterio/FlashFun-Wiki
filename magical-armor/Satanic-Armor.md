Satanic Armor is a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) set added in FlashFun.<br>
Netherite armor infused with demonic energy. Unbreakable, fire resistant, and packed with powerful enchantments. The full set lets you summon a fire pentagram by sneaking.

All four pieces are crafted **shapeless** using: Demonic Token + Infernal Essence + the corresponding Netherite armor piece.

## Set Bonus (Full Set)

- **Sneak** to summon a fire pentagram around you, igniting nearby entities for 5 seconds
- 10-second cooldown between activations
- Can be disabled per-player in settings

## Pieces

All pieces are **Unbreakable** and **Fire Resistant** (including lava).

### Satanic Helmet
- **Base:** Netherite Helmet + +2 Armor, +1 Toughness, +4 Max Health, +10% Knockback Resistance, +0.01 Speed
- **Enchantments:** Aqua Affinity III, Respiration V, Protection X, Thorns V
- **Unlock Level:** 20

### Satanic Chestplate
- **Base:** Netherite Chestplate + +4 Armor, +2 Toughness, +8 Max Health, +20% Knockback Resistance, +0.01 Speed, +10 Oxygen
- **Enchantments:** Protection X, Thorns V
- **Unlock Level:** 22

### Satanic Leggings
- **Base:** Netherite Leggings + +3 Armor, +1.5 Toughness, +4 Max Health, +15% Knockback Resistance, +0.015 Speed, +0.2 Water Movement
- **Enchantments:** Protection X, Thorns V
- **Unlock Level:** 21

### Satanic Boots
- **Base:** Netherite Boots + +1 Armor, +0.5 Toughness, +4 Max Health, +5% Knockback Resistance, +0.015 Speed
- **Enchantments:** Protection X, Thorns V, Depth Strider V, Soul Speed V, Feather Falling X
- **Unlock Level:** 18
