Rainbow Armor is a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) set added in FlashFun.<br>
Crafted from prismatic rainbow leather, the armor shifts color continuously while worn. Pieces reset to magenta when removed.

## Set Bonus

- **Rainbow Effect:** Each piece cycles through the full color spectrum while worn
- Pieces reset to magenta when taken off

## Pieces

| Piece | Armor | Unlock Level | Recipe |
|-|-|-|-|
| Rainbow Helmet | +2 | 8 | 3xRainbow Leather top row, gaps middle |
| Rainbow Chestplate | +3 | 10 | Standard chestplate shape |
| Rainbow Leggings | +2 | 9 | Standard leggings shape |
| Rainbow Boots | +1 | 7 | Standard boots shape |

All pieces use **Rainbow Leather** as the sole ingredient.
