The Hazmat Suit is a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) set added in FlashFun.<br>
Protective hazardous material gear. The chestplate is fire immune, the helmet provides underwater breathing. High-level crafting but strong utility.

## Piece Effects

Each piece provides its own passive independently - no full set bonus required.

### Hazmat Helmet
- **Armor:** +2
- **Effect:** Water Breathing (Respiration-style), filters toxic air
- **Unlock Level:** 28

### Hazmat Chestplate
- **Armor:** +3
- **Effect:** Fire immunity - immune to fire, fire ticks, lava, and hot floor damage
- **Unlock Level:** 32

### Hazmat Leggings
- **Armor:** +2
- **Effect:** Chemical resistant, full body coverage
- **Unlock Level:** 30

### Hazmat Boots
- **Armor:** +1
- **Unlock Level:** 26
