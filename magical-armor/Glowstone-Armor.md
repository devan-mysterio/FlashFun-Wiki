Glowstone Armor is a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) set added in FlashFun.<br>
Forged from concentrated luminescence. Wearing the full set grants permanent Night Vision - total darkness is no longer a concern.

## Set Bonus (Full Set)

- **Night Vision** - permanently active while all four pieces are worn

## Pieces

| Piece | Armor | Unlock Level |
|-|-|-|
| Glowstone Helmet | +2 | 7 |
| Glowstone Chestplate | +3 | 9 |
| Glowstone Leggings | +2 | 8 |
| Glowstone Boots | +1 | 6 |

All pieces use **Glowstone** blocks as the sole ingredient, in standard armor shapes.
