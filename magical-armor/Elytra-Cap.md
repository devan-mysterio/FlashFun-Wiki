The Elytra Cap is a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) helmet added in FlashFun.<br>
Forged from elytra scales and slime, this cap absorbs the damage from flying into walls at high speed. Wear it whenever you're on an elytra.

## Elytra Cap

- **Armor:** +2
- **Passive:** Cancels elytra crash damage (FLY_INTO_WALL) entirely
- **Unlock Level:** 14
