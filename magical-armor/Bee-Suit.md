The Bee Suit is a [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor) set added in FlashFun.<br>
Forged in honey and gold, blessed by the hive. The chestplate is a functional Elytra. The full set grants soft landings and improved jumping.

## Set Bonus (Full Set)

- **No fall damage**
- **Jump Boost II**
- **Slow Falling** when gliding and approaching the ground

## Pieces

All pieces have: **Protection II**, **Unbreaking IV**
