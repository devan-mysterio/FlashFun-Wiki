Ender Talismans (Tier II) are ender-infused upgrades to every [Talisman (Tier I)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans-Tier-1) added in FlashFun.<br>
Effects are identical to their Tier I counterparts - the upgrade is **activation range**: Ender Talismans work from your **Ender Chest** as well as your inventory.

## The Ender Upgrade

Regular Tier I talismans only activate when in your inventory or hotbar.
Ender Talismans also activate when stored in your **Ender Chest** - freeing up inventory space while keeping the passive effect active.

For consumable Ender Talismans, stacks in the Ender Chest are consumed first.

## Recipe (All Ender Talismans)

All 16 use the same shape - wrap the corresponding Tier I talisman with Ender Lumps:
