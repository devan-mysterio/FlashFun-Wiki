The Flask of Knowledge is a [Magical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Gadgets) added in FlashFun.<br>
A glass bottle imbued with magical energy. Fill it with a level of XP to store it, then drink it back later to reclaim that level.

## Flask of Knowledge

- **Yield:** 8 per craft (empty)
- **Unlock Level:** 3

## Usage

**Empty Flask → Full Flask**
Right-click while holding an empty Flask of Knowledge in open air (not on a block). Costs 1 XP level. The flask becomes a full Flask of Knowledge.

**Full Flask → XP**
Right-click while holding a full Flask of Knowledge. Grants 1 XP level.

Flasks stack to 64. You can carry large reserves of stored XP.
