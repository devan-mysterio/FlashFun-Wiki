The Magic Eye of Ender is a [Magical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Gadgets) added in FlashFun.<br>
A powerful artifact that lets you throw ender pearls on demand - but only while wearing full [Ender Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Armor).

## Magic Eye of Ender

- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 15
- **Cooldown:** 1 second

## Usage

Right-click while holding the Magic Eye of Ender. Shoots an ender pearl in the direction you're looking.

**Requirements:**
- Must be wearing all 4 pieces of Ender Armor (helmet, chestplate, leggings, boots)
- Without the full set, the item refuses to activate

Unlike vanilla ender pearls, this item does not consume itself on use.
