The Scroll of Dimensional Teleposition is a [Magical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Gadgets) added in FlashFun.<br>
A mystical scroll that creates a temporary black hole effect - rotating every nearby living entity 180° to face the opposite direction.

## Scroll of Dimensional Teleposition

- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 18

## Usage

Right-click while holding the scroll. All living entities within 10 blocks (except the caster, NPCs, and armor stands) are teleported in place with their yaw rotated 180°.

Affects players and mobs. Spawns portal particles on activation.
