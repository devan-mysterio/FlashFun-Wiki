The Elytra Scale is a [Magical Gadget](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Gadgets) crafting component added in FlashFun.<br>
A lightweight material infused with flight magic. Has no direct use - it exists to be crafted into other items.

## Elytra Scale

- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Unlock Level:** 28
