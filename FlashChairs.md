FlashChairs lets players sit on stairs and slabs by right-clicking them.

---

## How to Sit

**Right-click** any bottom stair or bottom slab with **empty hands**.

Requirements:
- Both hands must be empty
- You must not be sneaking
- The block above must be clear
- The seat must not already be occupied

To stand up, right-click again or jump.

---

## Supported Blocks

| Type | Condition |
|------|-----------|
| Stairs | Bottom half only |
| Slabs | Bottom placement only |

Top-half stairs and top-slab placements cannot be sat on.

---

## Behavior

- The chair is removed if the block is broken while someone is sitting on it
- Disconnecting while seated cleans up the chair automatically
- Player faces the direction they were looking when they sat down
