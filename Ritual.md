FlashFun has two distinct ritual systems, each with its own mechanics, requirements, and rewards.

---

## [Hellish Flame](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Hellish-Flame)

| Item | Unlock Level |
|------|-------------|
| Blank Rune | 4 |
| Infernal Essence | 5 |
| Air Rune | 6 |
| Earth Rune | 7 |
| Fire Rune | 8 |
| Water Rune | 9 |
| Ender Rune | 10 |
| Lightning Rune | 11 |
| Rainbow Rune | 12 |
| Essence of Afterlife | 13 |
| Gold Carat Bars (4–24) | 1–11 |

Items are thrown around a hellish flame as dark magic consumes and transforms them. Drop items on the ground near a fire, then right-click with Flint and Steel.

---

## [Demonic Ritual](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Demonic-Ritual)

A one-time summoning ritual that produces a **Demonic Token** - required to craft all Satanic items.

Fill a chest with the required offerings, ensure a 5x5 clear area, and sacrifice a Goat above the chest.
