The GPS Marker Tool is a waypoint tool in [GPS-Based Machines](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Machines) in FlashFun. It saves your current location as a named waypoint usable by any of your [GPS Teleporters](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Teleporter).

## GPS Marker Tool

- **Unlock Level:** 24
- **Yield:** 1 per craft

## Usage

**Right-click** while holding the GPS Marker Tool to save your current location as a waypoint. Waypoints are stored per-player and appear in the destination list of all your GPS Teleporters.

To delete a waypoint, open the [GPS Control Panel](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Teleporter#gps-control-panel) and right-click the waypoint entry.
