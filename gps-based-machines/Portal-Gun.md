The Portal Gun is a hand-held teleportation device in [GPS-Based Machines](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Machines) in FlashFun. It creates linked portal pairs - walk into one, exit the other.

## Portal Gun

- **Unlock Level:** 15
- **Yield:** 1 per craft

## Usage

**Left-click** to open the Portal Menu. From there you can create, name, and manage portals.

Use `/swp <portal name>` while looking at a location to save a portal destination. Use `/dwp <portal name>` to delete a saved destination.

Walking near a portal's entry point teleports you to its linked exit.
