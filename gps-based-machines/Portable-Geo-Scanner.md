The Portable Geo-Scanner is a tool in [GPS-Based Machines](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/GPS-Machines) in FlashFun. It scans your current chunk for underground oil reserves used by the [Combustion Generator](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Generators#combustion-generator) and [Oil Driller Minion](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Minions).

## Portable Geo-Scanner

- **Unlock Level:** 25
- **Cooldown:** 10 seconds

## Usage

**Right-click** while holding the Geo-Scanner. Results appear in your action bar showing the oil level and reserve size of the chunk you're standing in.

### Oil Level Results

| Level | Label | Reserve Size |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 0 | Nothing | - |
| 1 | Extremely Low | 300 - 700 buckets |
| 2 | Medium | 1,000 - 2,000 buckets |
| 3 | High | 2,500 - 4,500 buckets |
| 4 | Very High | 5,000 - 8,000 buckets |
| 5 | Infinite | Infinite |

Oil levels are randomly generated the first time a chunk is scanned and then permanently stored. Scan chance breakdown: 70% nothing, 15% extremely low, 9% medium, 4% high, ~2% very high, ~0.0001% infinite.
