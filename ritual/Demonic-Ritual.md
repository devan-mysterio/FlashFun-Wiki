The Demonic Ritual is a one-time summoning event in the [Ritual](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ritual) category of FlashFun. It has no unlock level - completing it drops a **Demonic Token**, which is required to craft all Satanic items.

---

## Requirements

### Chest Offerings

Place a chest and fill it with **exactly** the following items - no more, no less:

| Item | Quantity |
|------|----------|
| Bedrock | 64 |
| Netherite Ingot | 32 |
| Nether Star | 16 |
| Wither Skeleton Skull | 8 |
| Eternal Steak | 1 |
| Sculk Catalyst | 1 |

> **Eternal Steak** is a FlashFun crafted item. It must be obtained through the guide first.

### Area

The chest must have a completely clear **5x5x5** area around it - only the chest itself may be present. No blocks, no entities, nothing.

### The Sacrifice

A **Goat** must be killed directly above the chest. The goat must be within approximately one block of height above the chest when it dies.

---

## Performing the Ritual

1. Place the chest in an open area with 5x5 of clearance on all sides.
2. Load the chest with all required offerings in the exact quantities listed.
3. Spawn or lure a Goat so it is standing directly on top of the chest.
4. Kill the Goat.

---

## What Happens

Once the Goat is killed above the properly loaded chest:

- The chest disappears.
- The items float out of the chest and begin orbiting in a pentagram particle formation.
- The items spiral inward, accelerating as they converge.
- The floor beneath transforms - Magma Blocks and Netherrack spread outward in 7 organic veins with branching patterns.
- Five ritual mobs spawn in a ring around the ritual site:

| Mob | Base | Health | Equipment |
|-----|------|--------|-----------|
| Shadow Sentinel | Wither Skeleton | 50 HP | Full Netherite (Prot VIII / Sharpness X) |
| Infernal Ravager | Zombified Piglin | 25 HP | Full Gold (Sharpness X) |
| Ritual Flame | Blaze | 30 HP | - |

- On completion, the floor reverts to its original state and a **Demonic Token** drops at the ritual site.
