The Hellish Flame is a ritual crafting method in the [Ritual](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ritual) category of FlashFun. Items are thrown around a hellish flame as dark magic consumes and transforms them.

---

## How It Works

1. Place or find a **fire block** in the world.
2. **Drop** all required items on the ground near the fire - they must land within roughly 2 blocks of the flame.
3. At least **3 item entities** must be present near the fire at the same time.
4. **Right-click the fire with Flint and Steel**.
5. If a valid recipe is matched and you have it unlocked, the items are consumed and the output drops at the fire's location. The flame is extinguished.

> Items that don't match any recipe are left untouched - only the matching set is consumed.

## Gold Carat Bars

Gold refinement chain. Each bar requires **Gold Dust** and the previous tier's bar (except 4-Carat, which only needs Gold Dust).

| Output | Unlock Level | Ingredients |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| 4-Carat Gold Bar | 1 | Gold Dust |
| 6-Carat Gold Bar | 2 | Gold Dust + 4-Carat Gold Bar |
| 8-Carat Gold Bar | 3 | Gold Dust + 6-Carat Gold Bar |
| 10-Carat Gold Bar | 4 | Gold Dust + 8-Carat Gold Bar |
| 12-Carat Gold Bar | 5 | Gold Dust + 10-Carat Gold Bar |
| 14-Carat Gold Bar | 6 | Gold Dust + 12-Carat Gold Bar |
| 16-Carat Gold Bar | 7 | Gold Dust + 14-Carat Gold Bar |
| 18-Carat Gold Bar | 8 | Gold Dust + 14-Carat Gold Bar |
| 20-Carat Gold Bar | 9 | Gold Dust + 18-Carat Gold Bar |
| 22-Carat Gold Bar | 10 | Gold Dust + 20-Carat Gold Bar |
| 24-Carat Gold Bar | 11 | Gold Dust + 22-Carat Gold Bar |

> Both 16-Carat and 18-Carat branch from 14-Carat. 16-Carat is a dead end - the chain to 24-Carat runs through 18.
