The Organic System consists of two [Miscellaneous](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Miscellaneous) items added in FlashFun produced through electric machines.<br>
Raw crops go in the Food Fabricator and come out as Organic Food. Organic Food goes into the Food Composter and comes out as Organic Fertilizer.

## Organic Food

- **Yield:** 2 per craft
- **Machine:** Food Fabricator (Tier I or II)
- **Processing:** 10 seconds at 14 J/s
- **Unlock Level:** 18

### Recipe (Machine)

| | |
|-|-|
| Tin Can | Crop (any valid type) |

**Valid crop types:** Wheat, Carrot, Potato, Wheat Seeds, Beetroot, Melon Slice, Apple, Sweet Berries, Dried Kelp, Cocoa Beans, Seagrass

The output is labeled with the crop type - e.g., "Organic Food (Carrot)".

## Organic Fertilizer

- **Yield:** 2 per craft
- **Machine:** Food Composter (Tier I or II)
- **Processing:** 10 seconds at 16 J/s
- **Unlock Level:** 20

### Recipe (Machine)

| |
|-|
| Organic Food (any type) |

The output is labeled with the original crop type - e.g., "Organic Fertilizer (Carrot)".

*Crop goes in can. Can goes in machine. Machine produces food. Food goes in another machine. Fertilizer comes out. Agriculture.*
