Duct Tape is a [Miscellaneous](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Miscellaneous) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Right-click a damaged anvil to repair it by one stage of damage. Each piece of duct tape fixes one stage - a heavily damaged anvil needs up to two uses.

## Duct Tape

- **Yield:** 2 per craft
- **Usage:** Right-click a damaged anvil to repair one damage stage
- **Unlock Level:** 10
