FlashReplant automatically replants crops when you break them, as long as you have seeds in your inventory and the crop is fully grown.

---

## How It Works

Break a fully grown crop. If you have the matching seed in your inventory, one seed is consumed and the crop is immediately replanted in the same spot.

The crop must be **fully grown** - breaking immature crops does nothing.

---

## Supported Crops

| Crop | Seed Required |
|------|--------------|
| Wheat | Wheat Seeds |
| Beetroot | Beetroot Seeds |
| Carrots | Carrot |
| Potatoes | Potato |


*Break crop, replant crop. Farming automation for people who couldn't be bothered to crouch.*
