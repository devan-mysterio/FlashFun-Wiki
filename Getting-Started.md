If you have just started using FlashFun it may seem overwhelming or very complicated.
You may not know where to start.
If that sounds familiar then you have come to the right place!

## 1. The FlashFun Guide
The very first thing you will need on your journey is the **FlashFun Guide**.
Type `/ff guide` to obtain it and Right-Click the book to open it.
You will now be presented with all the Categories FlashFun has to offer.
In each Category you will find Items that FlashFun has added.
You can click on any Item to see its Recipe.
Take a moment to browse through the guide.

## 2. Unlocking Recipes
You will notice that a lot of FlashFun Items are locked.
This means you have to unlock the recipe before you can craft or use it.
To unlock an Item, simply click on it in your Guide while it shows as **LOCKED**.
Unlocking Items will cost you XP levels, so make sure to stock up on Experience before diving in.

## 3. The FlashFun Crafting Altar
The very first item you will need to craft is the **FlashFun Crafting Altar** - it is the backbone of almost everything else in FlashFun.

Craft it in a regular Minecraft Crafting Table using this recipe:

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Nether Star | Amethyst Block | Nether Star |
| Amethyst Block | **Crafting Table** | Amethyst Block |
| Nether Star | Amethyst Block | Nether Star |

Once placed, Right-Click the Altar to open the FlashFun crafting interface.
This is where the majority of FlashFun recipes are crafted.
You can read more about this [here](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Crafting-Altar).

## 4. Collecting Resources
A lot of recipes will require various dusts, ingots, and alloys.
A good place to start is crafting a [Gold Pan](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Useful-Items) at the Crafting Altar.
Right-Click Dirt or Gravel with your Gold Pan to collect dust and raw materials.
These dusts can then be fed into a [Smeltery Minion](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Minions) to be smelted into Ingots, or processed further down the line.

## 5. Minions
As you progress, you will want to automate your resource gathering.
FlashFun uses **Minions** instead of multiblock machines - small automated workers you can place in the world to mine, smelt, pan, drill, and more.
There are 11 types of Minions, each specializing in a different task.
You can read more about them [here](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Minions).

## 6. Electric Machines
Once you have resources coming in, it's time to build your first Electric Machines.
These machines require an energy source (such as a Coal Generator or Solar Generator) connected through the energy network.
They can automate enchanting, breeding, smelting, freezing, and much more.
You can read more about them [here](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Energy-and-Electricity).

## 7. Enjoy the plugin
Now you should have everything you need to start your FlashFun adventure.
Have fun, and enjoy Flash-Networks!
