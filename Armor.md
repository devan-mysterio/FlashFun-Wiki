Armor sets are craftable armor added in FlashFun that provide stronger or unique protection beyond vanilla armor.<br>
Unlike [Magical Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical-Armor), these sets have no special effects - they are pure protection upgrades.
