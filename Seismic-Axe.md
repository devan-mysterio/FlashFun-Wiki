The Seismic Axe is a [Weapon](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Weapons) added in FlashFun.<br>
Right-clicking unleashes a 7-block radius shockwave that launches nearby blocks as falling blocks and sends enemies flying into the air.

---

## Seismic Axe

- **Shockwave Radius:** 7 blocks
- **Entity Damage:** 4
- **Durability Cost:** 3 per use
- **Cooldown:** 5 seconds
- **Unlock Level:** 20

### How to Use

Right-click to trigger the shockwave. Any blocks within 7 blocks are launched upward as falling blocks. Nearby entities (except you) get sent skyward and take 4 damage on activation.

> **Note:** This costs 3 durability per use, so keep an eye on the tool's condition.
