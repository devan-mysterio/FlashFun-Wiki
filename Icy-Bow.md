The Icy Bow is a [Weapon](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Weapons) added in FlashFun.<br>
Freezes hit targets solid and slows their movement. Also extinguishes any fire on them, which is oddly considerate.

---

## Icy Bow

- **On Hit:** Extends freeze ticks beyond player max, applies Slowness III for 4 seconds
- **Also extinguishes fire on targets**
- **Unlock Level:** 22
