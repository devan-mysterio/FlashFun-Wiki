Enhanced Furnaces are a series of [Basic Machines](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Basic-Machines) added in FlashFun.<br>
Upgradeable furnaces that smelt faster, use fuel more efficiently, and have a luck multiplier for bonus outputs. Each tier upgrades the previous one.

All tiers I-XI share the same recipe shape: ` S ` / `BFH` / ` E ` where S=Steel Ingot, B=Basic Circuit Board, F=Previous Furnace, H=Heating Coil, E=Electric Motor.

## Reinforced Furnace XII

**10x speed, 10x fuel efficiency, 3x luck** - a massive jump from tier XI.

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|

| Heating Coil | Enhanced Furnace XI | Heating Coil |
