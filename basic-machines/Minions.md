Minions are a category of [Basic Machines](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Basic-Machines) added in FlashFun.<br>
Placeable worker units that automate various tasks. Right-click to place one in the world, then interact with it to configure inputs and outputs.

## Sifter Minion

- **Function:** Automatically sifts items
- **Unlock Level:** 10

- **Function:** Mines all ores within its chunk automatically

## Oil Driller Minion

- **Function:** Drills for oil in its chunk
- **Fuel:** Consumes 1 Coal per bucket of oil produced
- **Output:** Rate depends on the chunk's oil level
- **Unlock Level:** 25
