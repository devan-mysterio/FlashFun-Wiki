Hardened Glass is a [Technical Component](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Technical-Components) added in FlashFun.<br>
Glass reinforced with a Reinforced Plate core. Blast resistant and placeable, but not immune to Wither explosions. Used as an ingredient in Wither-Proof materials.

## Hardened Glass

- **Used in:** Wither-Proof Obsidian, Wither-Proof Glass
- **Blast resistant** against normal explosions
- **Not immune** to Wither explosion damage
- **Unlock Level:** 18

### Recipe

|-|-|-|
| Glass | Glass | Glass |
| Glass | Reinforced Plate | Glass |
| Glass | Glass | Glass |
