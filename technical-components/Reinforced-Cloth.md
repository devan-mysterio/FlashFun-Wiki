Reinforced Cloth is a [Technical Component](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Technical-Components) added in FlashFun.<br>
Heavy-duty fabric reinforced with lead to provide radiation shielding. Used to craft the Hazmat Suit.

## Reinforced Cloth

- **Yield:** 2 per craft
- **Used in:** Hazmat Suit (all four pieces)
- **Unlock Level:** 18
