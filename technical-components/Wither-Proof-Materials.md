Wither-Proof Materials are [Technical Components](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Technical-Components) added in FlashFun.<br>
Lead-reinforced blocks that are immune to all explosion damage including Wither skull explosions. Use them to build structures that need to survive a Wither encounter.

## Wither-Proof Obsidian

- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Immune to:** All explosions, including Wither damage
- **Unlock Level:** 22

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Lead Ingot | Obsidian | Lead Ingot |
| Obsidian | Hardened Glass | Obsidian |
| Lead Ingot | Obsidian | Lead Ingot |

## Wither-Proof Glass

- **Yield:** 4 per craft
- **Immune to:** All explosions, including Wither damage
- **Unlock Level:** 24

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Lead Ingot | Wither-Proof Obsidian | Lead Ingot |
| Wither-Proof Obsidian | Hardened Glass | Wither-Proof Obsidian |
| Lead Ingot | Wither-Proof Obsidian | Lead Ingot |

*The Wither has met its match. Its match is lead, obsidian, and stubbornness.*
