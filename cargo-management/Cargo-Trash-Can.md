The Cargo Trash Can is part of [Cargo Management](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Cargo-Management) in FlashFun. It destroys any items delivered to it through the cargo network.

## Cargo Trash Can

- **Unlock Level:** 24
- **Yield:** 1 per craft

## Usage

Place the Cargo Trash Can as a block. Any items pushed to it via a Cargo Node (Output) on its channel are permanently destroyed. A hologram above it shows current throughput.

Accepts items from all sides. Useful for disposing of excess byproducts from processing machines or reactors.
