The Auto Crafter is part of [Cargo Management](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Cargo-Management) in FlashFun. It automatically crafts items using ingredients supplied by a cargo network.

## Auto Crafter

- **Unlock Level:** 28
- **Yield:** 1 per craft

## Usage

Place the Auto Crafter as a block and right-click to configure a crafting recipe. Connect it to a cargo network - input nodes supply ingredients, output nodes collect the results.

Works with all standard crafting recipes.
