The Reactor Access Port is part of [Cargo Management](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Cargo-Management) in FlashFun. It allows cargo nodes to interact with a [Nuclear Reactor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Reactors#nuclear-reactor) or [Nether Star Reactor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Reactors#nether-star-reactor).

## Reactor Access Port

- **Unlock Level:** 38
- **Yield:** 1 per craft

## Usage

Place the Reactor Access Port **3 blocks directly above** a reactor. It acts as a buffer between the reactor and the cargo network.

- **Input nodes** can supply fuel and coolant cells to the reactor
- **Output nodes** can extract byproducts (e.g. Neptunium from a Nuclear Reactor)

Without a Reactor Access Port, cargo nodes cannot interact with reactors.
