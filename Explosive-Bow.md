The Explosive Bow is a [Weapon](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Weapons) added in FlashFun.<br>
Launches hit targets into the air and sets them on fire on impact.

---

## Explosive Bow

- **On Hit:** Launches target upward (random horizontal velocity up to 0.5, vertical 1.2 - 1.6)
- **Fire Duration:** 3 seconds on hit
- **Unlock Level:** 22
