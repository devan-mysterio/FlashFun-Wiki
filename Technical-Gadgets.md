Technical Gadgets are electricity-powered wearable items and handheld tools added in FlashFun.<br>
Most require charge (measured in Joules) to operate. Charge items at a Charging Bench or use a [Solar Helmet](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Solar-Helmet) for passive charging.

## Multitools

Handheld multi-function tool. Sneak + right-click to cycle modes. Right-click to use current mode.

| Tier | Unlock Level | Max Charge | Modes |
|-|-|-|-|
| [T1–T6](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Multitool) | 22–36 | 20–75 J | Magic Eye, Wind, Grapple, Portable Craft |
| [T7](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Multitool) | 42 | 100 J | All modes + Gold Pan, Nether Gold Pan, Water, Storm |
