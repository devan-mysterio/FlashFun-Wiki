Horse Armor is an [Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Armor) item added in FlashFun.<br>
Craftable variants of the three tiers of vanilla horse armor. Equip on your horse via its inventory.

## Horse Armor

| Variant | Unlock Level |
|-|-|
| Iron Horse Armor | 5 |
| Golden Horse Armor | 6 |
| Diamond Horse Armor | 8 |
