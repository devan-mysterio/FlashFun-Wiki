Cactus Armor is an [Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Armor) set added in FlashFun.<br>
Leather armor dyed green and reinforced with cactus. Thorns III punishes anyone who hits you.

## Cactus Armor

- **Enchantments:** Thorns III, Unbreaking IV
- **Base Material:** Leather (green dyed)

| Piece | Unlock Level |
|-|-|
| Helmet | 10 |
| Chestplate | 14 |
| Leggings | 12 |
| Boots | 8 |
