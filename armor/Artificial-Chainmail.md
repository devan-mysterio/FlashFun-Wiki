Artificial Chainmail is an [Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Armor) set added in FlashFun.<br>
Chainmail armor crafted through magical means - equivalent to vanilla chainmail in stats, but actually obtainable.

## Artificial Chainmail

- **Enchantments:** None
- **Base Material:** Chainmail

| Piece | Unlock Level | Ingredient |
|-|-|-|
| Helmet | 4 | Steel Chain |
| Chestplate | 5 | Steel Chain |
| Leggings | 5 | Iron Chain |
| Boots | 3 | Iron Chain |
