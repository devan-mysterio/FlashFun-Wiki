Magical Utility items are part of the [Magical](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical) category in FlashFun. They cover cures, fertilizer, knowledge transfer, enchanting, and structural crafting.

---

## Magical Zombie Pills

Instantly cures Zombie Villagers and Zombified Piglins - no golden apple or weakness potion ritual required afterward.

- **Unlock Level:** 22
- **Yield:** 2 per craft

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| 4-Carat Gold Bar | Magical Lump (Tier 2) | 4-Carat Gold Bar |
| Apple | Weakness Potion | Apple |
| 4-Carat Gold Bar | Magical Lump (Tier 2) | 4-Carat Gold Bar |

Resets a villager's job. On failure, may clear all trades or convert the villager to a nitwit.

- **Unlock Level:** 16
- **Yield:** 3 per craft
