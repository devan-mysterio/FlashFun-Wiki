Magical Components are crafting ingredients in the [Magical](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical) category in FlashFun. They are used in staffs, talismans, runes, and advanced magical recipes.

---

## Magical Book Cover

Used to craft the Elemental Staff and the Tome of Knowledge Sharing.

- **Unlock Level:** 6
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
