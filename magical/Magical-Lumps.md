Magical Lumps and Ender Lumps are the core crafting ingredients of the [Magical](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical) category in FlashFun. All magical recipes trace back to these six items.

Each tier is crafted by combining four of the previous tier. All lump recipes are **shapeless** - no grid placement required.

---

## Magical Lump (Tier 1)

- **Unlock Level:** 1
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Shapeless:** 1x Nether Wart

---

## Magical Lump (Tier 2)

- **Unlock Level:** 3
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Shapeless:** 4x Magical Lump (Tier 1)

---

## Magical Lump (Tier 3)

- **Unlock Level:** 6
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Shapeless:** 4x Magical Lump (Tier 2)

---

## Ender Lump (Tier 1)

- **Unlock Level:** 2
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Shapeless:** 1x Ender Eye

---

## Ender Lump (Tier 2)

- **Unlock Level:** 4
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Shapeless:** 4x Ender Lump (Tier 1)

---

## Ender Lump (Tier 3)

- **Unlock Level:** 7
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
- **Shapeless:** 4x Ender Lump (Tier 2)
