Magical Flight items are part of the [Magical](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical) category in FlashFun. They provide craftable wings, item attraction, and an automated item collection hopper.

---

## Elytra

A craftable elytra - no End ship required.

- **Unlock Level:** 28
- **Yield:** 1 per craft

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Elytra Scale | Air Rune | Elytra Scale |
| Air Rune | Leather Chestplate | Air Rune |
| Elytra Scale | Air Rune | Elytra Scale |

---

## Infused Elytra

- **Unlock Level:** 35

## Infused Magnet

Attracts dropped items within 6 blocks while in your inventory. Hold Shift to activate.

- **Unlock Level:** 24
- **Yield:** 1 per craft

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Magical Lump (Tier 3) | - | Magical Lump (Tier 3) |
| Ender Lump (Tier 2) | Magnet | Ender Lump (Tier 2) |
| Magical Lump (Tier 3) | - | Magical Lump (Tier 3) |

---

## Infused Hopper

An enhanced hopper that attracts items within a 7x7x7 area and teleports them directly inside.

- **Unlock Level:** 28
- **Yield:** 1 per craft

### Recipe

| | | |
|-|-|-|
| Obsidian | Earth Rune | Hopper |
| Ender Rune | Infused Magnet | Ender Rune |
| Hopper | Earth Rune | Obsidian |

*Craftable wings, magnetic inventory, and a hopper that cheats physics. Totally balanced.*
