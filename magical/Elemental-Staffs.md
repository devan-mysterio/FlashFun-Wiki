Elemental Staffs are weapons and tools in the [Magical](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magical) category in FlashFun. The Elemental Staff is the base component used to craft all four elemental variants.

---

## Elemental Staff

The base staff used as a crafting ingredient for all elemental staffs.

- **Unlock Level:** 12
- **Yield:** 1 per craft
