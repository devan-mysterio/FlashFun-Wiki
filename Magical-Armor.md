Magical Armor sets are enchanted armor added in FlashFun that provide special effects and set bonuses beyond vanilla protection.<br>
Most sets grant passive effects when wearing the full four-piece set.

| Set | Unlock Level | Set Bonus |
|-|-|-|
| [Rainbow Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Rainbow-Armor) | 7–10 | Color cycles through the rainbow while worn |
| [Glowstone Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Glowstone-Armor) | 6–9 | Permanent Night Vision |
| [Slime Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Slime-Armor) | 9–12 | Jump Boost II, Speed I, no fall damage |
| [Ender Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Armor) | 11–14 | Immune to ender pearl damage, erratic teleport in rain |
| [Reinforced Slime Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Reinforced-Slime-Armor) | 14–17 | Jump Boost II, Speed I, no fall damage |
| [Elytra Cap](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Elytra-Cap) | 14 | Cancels elytra crash damage (helmet only) |
| [Bee Suit](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Bee-Suit) | 15–18 | Jump Boost II, Slow Falling on descent, no fall damage |
| [Satanic Armor](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Satanic-Armor) | 18–22 | Sneak to summon a fire pentagram, fire immune |
| [Hazmat Suit](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Hazmat-Suit) | 26–32 | Fire immunity (chestplate), Water Breathing (helmet) |
