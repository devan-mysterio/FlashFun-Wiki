Magical Gadgets are handheld magical items added in FlashFun with active right-click abilities or utility as crafting components.<br>
Unlike talismans, these are used directly - right-click to activate their effect.

## Gadgets

| Item | Unlock Level | Use |
|-|-|-|
| [Flask of Knowledge](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Flask-of-Knowledge) | 3 | Store and retrieve XP levels |
| [Magic Eye of Ender](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Magic-Eye-of-Ender) | 15 | Shoot ender pearls without taking damage (requires Ender Armor) |
| [Scroll of Dimensional Teleposition](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Scroll-of-Dimensional-Teleposition) | 18 | Rotate all nearby entities 180° |
| [Elytra Scale](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Elytra-Scale) | 28 | Crafting component for advanced items |
