FlashSpirits is a custom brewing system that adds craftable alcoholic drinks to Flash-Networks. Drinks are brewed through a multi-step process using the brewing stand, with each recipe requiring several ingredient stages to complete.

---

## How Brewing Works

Each drink has multiple tiers. You brew it step by step in a brewing stand - the output of each tier becomes the ingredient for the next. Complete all tiers to produce the final drink.

Use `/brewingguide` to open the in-game brewing recipe book.

## Getting Drunk

The more you drink, the higher your blood alcohol level. Higher potency drinks raise it faster. At high enough levels your chat starts slurring, vision effects kick in, and you may puke.

Different drinks produce different puke colors - normal, bloody, toxic, or rainbow depending on what you drank.

Use `/bac` to check your current blood alcohol level.
