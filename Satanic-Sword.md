The Satanic Sword is a [Weapon](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Weapons) added in FlashFun.<br>
Forged from a Netherite Sword infused with dark energy. Unbreakable, fire immune, and loaded with enchantments that have no business being on a single item.

---

## Satanic Sword

- **Attack Damage:** +10.0
- **Attack Speed:** -2.4
- **Unbreakable:** Yes
- **Fire Immune:** Yes
- **Unlock Level:** 14
