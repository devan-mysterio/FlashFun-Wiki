Frequently asked questions about FlashFun and Flash-Networks.



## I have an error/bug with FlashFun

Head over to the `#tickets` channel in the [Flash-Networks Discord](https://discord.gg/WTspjB2b6g) and open a ticket.

You don't need to worry about plugin versions - just tell us:
1. Did anything show up in your chat when it happened?
2. If not, describe what you were doing and what went wrong as clearly as you can.

That's it. Someone will help you out.



## What's the difference between a Talisman and an Ender Talisman?

Regular [Talismans](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Talismans) must be in your inventory to activate.<br>
[Ender Talismans](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans) work from either your inventory or your Ender Chest - whichever one has it.



## Can you stack Talismans?

Talismans are stackable items but having more than one of the same type will **not** increase their effects - you won't get double the luck by carrying two.<br>
However, some Talismans are consumed on use, so having extras means they'll last you longer.



## What can I do with Stone Chunks?

4 Stone Chunks can be fed into a [Compressor Minion](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Minions) to produce one Cobblestone.



## How do I disable items or machines per-world?

You can't. FlashFun doesn't have per-world item restrictions or any admin toggles for disabling individual items.<br>
If something needs to be restricted, talk to Devan.

## How do I repair FlashFun items?

Use an [Auto Anvil](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Basic-Machines).<br>
The Auto Anvil will handle the repair automatically.



## Can I enchant FlashFun items?

Yes - use the [Auto Enchanter](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Basic-Machines) or remove enchants with the [Auto Disenchanter](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Basic-Machines).<br>
Regular enchanting tables won't work on most FlashFun items.

> **Note:** The Auto Enchanter and Auto Disenchanter do **not** work on **Satanic** or **ArchAngel** items. These item sets are too powerful for that kind of tinkering.



## How long does coolant last in reactors?

One coolant cell is consumed every **10 seconds** of active reactor operation, regardless of coolant type.

Here is a full breakdown of fuel durations and how much coolant each one requires:

**Nuclear Reactor** (uses Reactor Coolant Cells - 500 J/s output)

| Fuel | Duration | Coolant Required | Byproduct |
| ---- | -------- | ---------------- | --------- |
| Plutonium | 5 min (300s) | 30 cells | None |
| Neptunium | 10 min (600s) | 60 cells | Plutonium |
| Uranium | 20 min (1200s) | 120 cells | None |
| Boosted Uranium | 25 min (1500s) | 150 cells | Neptunium |

**Nether Star Reactor** (uses Nether Ice Coolant Cells - 1024 J/s output)

| Fuel | Duration | Coolant Required | Byproduct |
| ---- | -------- | ---------------- | --------- |
| Nether Star | 30 min (1800s) | 180 cells | None |

> **Note:** Reactors in Generator Mode will pause when the energy network is full, consuming no fuel or coolant while idle. Switch to Production Mode if you want continuous operation for byproduct generation.
