Talismans (Tier I) are magical accessories added in FlashFun that provide passive bonuses when held in inventory or consumed for instant effects.<br>
Keep them in your inventory or hotbar - most work automatically. Consumable talismans are spent on activation.

See also: [Ender Talismans (Tier II)](https://github.com/devan-mysterio/FlashFun-Wiki/wiki/Ender-Talismans-Tier-2) - ender-infused upgrades to each talisman
